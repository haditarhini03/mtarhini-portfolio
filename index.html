Background

Mohamad Tarhini is an engineer, entrepreneur, and investor focused on turning innovation into measurable impact.

With over 20 years of international experience across the Middle East, Europe, and Latin America, he works at the intersection of sustainability, technology, and venture creation.

As Founder and Managing Partner of DAIS Group, he leads integrated consulting, engineering, and digital solutions across environment, energy, cities, and agriculture, supporting governments, institutions, and organizations in delivering resilient, future-ready outcomes.

He is also Co-Founder of NUWA Ventures, a hybrid venture studio and investment platform scaling technology-driven solutions in Agri-Tech, Clean Tech, Health & Wellbeing, and EdTech.

Guided by a belief that sustainability and innovation drive lasting progress, Mohamad connects policy with practice and ideas with real-world impact.
